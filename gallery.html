"Training with Titan Athletics for the past five years has been one of the best decisions I've made as an athlete. Under the guidance of Coach Sumesh, I've grown not only as a sprinter but also as a person. His dedication, knowledge, and belief in me have helped me achieve milestones I once only dreamed of, including recently breaking the 11-second barrier in the 100m."

"Training with Titans for the past 6–7 years has completely shaped my athletic career. Sir’s depth of knowledge and endless patience have helped me drop my 100m personal best to a 10.71s. Thanks to his coaching, I’ve consistently finished on the podiums winning CBSE National Gold medals in the 100m, 200m, and relay, alongside multiple UAE National Gold medals and All India Junior National finals. If you want an environment that turns hard work into championship results, this is it."

— Nareshkumar Selvam, Elite
